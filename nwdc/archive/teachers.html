Teachers

Gen Kelsang Jampa has studied and practiced Kadampa Buddhism for many years under the guidance of Venerable Geshe Kelsang Gyatso. He originally studied at KMC Washington and was directly involved in the development of the Center. Gen Jampa is the US NKT-IKBU National Spiritual Director and the Resident Teacher of Kadampa Meditation Center Texas.

 



Gen Kelsang Khedrub is Resident Teacher of Kadampa Meditation Center Washington in Seattle. He will give the introductory talk on Friday evening.

 

Kelsang Tsoglam is Resident Teacher of Olympia Mahayana Buddhist Center in Olympia, WA. She will lead the meditations during the Celebration.
