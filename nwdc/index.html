Everyone Welcome

Don't miss this very meaningful weekend — the annual gathering of Kadampa Buddhists from the Northwest at Kadampa Meditation Center Washington. While you are here, enjoy transformative teachings, guided meditations, and beautiful prayers for peace. Between sessions spend time connecting with like-minded friends in the Temple cafe, gift shop and bookstore.

Everyone, whether new to Buddhism or an experienced practitioner, is welcome!
