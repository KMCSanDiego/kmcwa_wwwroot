Program

The annual Northwest Dharma Celebration is a wonderful opportunity for people throughout the Northwest region to gather together to receive practical, inspiring teachings and meet spiritual friends.

This year Gen Kelsang Jampa, our US National Spiritual Director for the NKT-IKBU, will offer the empowerment of the Wisdom Buddha Je Tsongkhapa and provide instructions on the profound practice of Heart Jewel. The Guru Yoga of Je Tsongkhapa can be practiced in accordance with Sutra, Action Tantra or Highest Yoga Tantra. Gen Jampa will give commentary to this practice and help us to increase our wisdom and gain profound protection along our spiritual path.

The Celebration will be held at KMC Washington – a Kadampa Temple in Seattle dedicated to World Peace which arose from the vision of Venerable Geshe Kelsang Gyatso, the Founder of the New Kadampa Tradition, as part of the International Temples Project (ITP).
